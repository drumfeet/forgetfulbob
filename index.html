About

Forgetful Bob started in 2014 in Cebu City, Philippines.

Dominic Dosdos (vocals/guitar) continues the music.

Inactive members: Ethan Ronoel Salazar (drums), Jess Tamboboy (guitar), Deniel Molina (bass), Michael Tubato (bass).
