About

▼

Forgetful Bob is an indie rock band from Cebu City, Philippines, formed in 2018. The band consists of Dominic Dosdos (vocals/guitar), Ethan Ronoel Salazar (guitar), Jess Tamboboy (bass), Deniel Molina (drums), and Michael Tubato (keyboards).

Drawing inspiration from a diverse range of influences, Forgetful Bob creates a unique sound that blends nostalgic melodies with introspective lyrics. Their music explores themes of memory, longing, and the human experience, inviting listeners on an emotional journey through their soundscapes.

The band released their debut album "SA YO NA RA" in 2019, which received critical acclaim in the local music scene. They continue to perform at venues across the Philippines, connecting with fans through their passionate live performances.
